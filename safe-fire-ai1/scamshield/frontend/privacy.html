Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you use ScamShield AI, we may collect:

  • Mobile number (for OTP authentication)
  • Email address (optional, for Google OAuth or newsletter)
  • Text content you submit for analysis (anonymized)
  • IP address (for rate limiting and fraud prevention)
  • Usage data (scan counts, verdicts — no personal content)

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide scam detection services
  • To improve our detection models (anonymized data only)
  • To send OTP verification codes
  • To send weekly safety digests (if opted in)
  • To prevent abuse and enforce rate limits

3. Data Storage & Security

All data is stored in encrypted databases. We use industry-standard security practices including HTTPS, JWT authentication, and hashed OTPs. We do not store raw OTPs — only hashed versions.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share anonymized, aggregated scam pattern data with law enforcement agencies (CERT-In, cybercrime.gov.in) to help combat fraud.

5. Your Rights

  • Access your data via the Dashboard
  • Delete your account and all associated data at any time
  • Opt out of newsletter at any time
  • Request data export by contacting hello@scamshield.ai

6. Cookies

We use localStorage (not cookies) to store your authentication token and preferences. No third-party tracking cookies are used.

7. Children's Privacy

ScamShield AI is intended for users 13 years and older. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

8. Contact

For privacy concerns: privacy@scamshield.ai
