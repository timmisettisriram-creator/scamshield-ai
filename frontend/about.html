Frequently Asked Questions

How does ScamShield detect scams? ▼
We combine NLP analysis, URL reputation checks, email domain intelligence, OCR for screenshots, and a community-powered Trust Graph to give you a comprehensive risk score.
Is my data stored or shared? ▼
Scanned text is anonymized and used only to improve detection. We never sell personal data. You can delete your account and all data at any time.
Is ScamShield free to use? ▼
Yes, the core analyzer is completely free. We plan to offer a premium tier for businesses and HR teams in the future.
What languages does it support? ▼
English, Hindi, Hinglish, Tamil, Telugu, and Bengali. We're actively expanding to more Indian languages.
How accurate is the detection? ▼
Our model achieves ~94% accuracy on our test dataset. However, always use your own judgment — no AI is 100% perfect.
Can I report a scam I encountered? ▼
Yes! After any scan, you can submit it to our community feed. You can also report directly at cybercrime.gov.in or call 1930.
Does it work on WhatsApp screenshots? ▼
Yes. Upload a screenshot using the OCR tab and our engine will extract the text and analyze it automatically.
What is the Trust Graph? ▼
A visual network map linking phone numbers, UPI IDs, and domains that have appeared in reported scams — helping you see if a contact is connected to known fraud.
How do I integrate ScamShield into my app? ▼
We offer a REST API. Contact us at api@scamshield.ai for access and documentation.
Who should I call if I've already been scammed? ▼
Call the National Cyber Crime Helpline at 1930 immediately. Also file a report at cybercrime.gov.in. Act fast — early reporting improves recovery chances.
